About

Our Mission

BTC Beef is redefining the beef industry through peer-to-peer connections and decentralized food distribution. We deliver high-quality beef directly to our customers, embracing sound money principles and supporting a sustainable, circular economy.

  • Premium Quality
  • Unmatched Affordability
  • Community Focused

When you order from us, you can be confident that every cut of beef comes from carefully raised Red Angus cattle, managed with regenerative and sustainable rotational grazing practices on our farm in Caledonia, Ontario.

Why Choose BTC BEEF?

We raise premium, grass-fed beef on our local farm and deliver it straight to your door. Every cut is hand-prepared to a full 1-inch thickness, ensuring rich flavor, tenderness, and a true farm-to-table experience.
